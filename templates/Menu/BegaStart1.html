Horse Racing

Horse Racing is a betting game where you need to bet on a horse at the racing.

Every horse have different speeds and all horses may stumble and die.

You can bet on 1-5 horses and you can bet that all horses will die or survive.

If you bet on horse and it wins coefficient will be X5.

If you bet that all horses wil die and you win coefficient will be X10.

If you bet that all horses wil survive and you win coefficient will be X2.
